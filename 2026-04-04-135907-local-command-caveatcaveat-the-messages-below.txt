  Key details:

  filenames, each tagged with a category (data-category).
  - Category filters read ?cat= from the URL and show/hide products + highlight
  the active filter.

   by thumbnail clicks and ←/→ keys.
  - Fonts loaded locally via @font-face — Avant Garde Bold for headings/nav
  labels, Adobe Clean (regular + italic) for body.
  - Responsive — sidebar collapses to a hamburger overlay at 768px; grid goes
  3→2→1 columns.
